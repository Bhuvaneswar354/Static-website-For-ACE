About ACE

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To foster innovation and excellence in computer engineering by providing students with opportunities to learn, collaborate, and create cutting-edge technological solutions that address real-world challenges.

Our Vision

To be the premier student organization that bridges the gap between academic learning and industry requirements, producing skilled computer engineers who lead technological advancement.

Our Objectives

  • Organize technical workshops and seminars
  • Conduct hackathons and coding competitions
  • Facilitate industry-academia interactions
  • Promote research and innovation
  • Build a strong alumni network
